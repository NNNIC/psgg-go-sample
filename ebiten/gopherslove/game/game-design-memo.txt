- Game Concept 
  
  It is like a block game.
  Mutiple Nics appear around center screen.
  A Gopher alwyas moves.
  If A Gopher hits Nic, Nic will be disapeard.
  Gopher can be touched to change its direction to move.

    - Create Stage

  GameNicControl

  GameGopherControl
